Ministry

As a member of our Women’s Ministry, you will find a warm and welcoming group of women who are passionate about supporting each other and growing in faith together. We offer events and outreach programs, providing opportunities to connect with other women, build relationships, and deepen our understanding of God’s Word. We believe that every woman has a unique purpose and calling in life, and our ministry seeks to empower and encourage women to live out their full potential in Christ. Come join us as we share life’s joys and struggles, grow in our faith and serve our community together.
